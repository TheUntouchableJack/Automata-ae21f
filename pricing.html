Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about pricing

What is a "customer"?

A customer is someone who joins your loyalty program. Each unique email or phone number counts as one customer, regardless of how many visits they make.

What does Royal AI do?

Royal is your AI marketing assistant. On Free, you get 20 queries/month to taste the AI. On Pro, Royal runs autonomously — learning your business, creating automations, and sending campaigns without asking. On Max, Royal also proves ROI by tracking which campaigns drive store visits.

Do I need a credit card to start?

No. You can start with our free plan or begin a 14-day trial of any paid plan without entering a credit card. Upgrade when you're ready.

What's the difference between LTD and subscriptions?

AppSumo Lifetime Deal (LTD) includes the loyalty platform and email campaigns forever. Subscription plans add Royal AI, SMS campaigns, and more features. LTD users can add Royalty Pro ($79/mo) to unlock full Royal AI, SMS, and white-label branding.

Can I change plans anytime?

Yes! Upgrade or downgrade at any time. When upgrading, you'll be charged the prorated difference. When downgrading, you'll receive credit toward future billing.

What's white-label branding?

White-label removes "Powered by Royalty" branding from your customer-facing loyalty app. Your customers see your brand only.

Is there a money-back guarantee?

Yes! We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on subscriptions. AppSumo deals have a 60-day refund policy through their platform.

Can I stack AppSumo codes?

Yes! Each code increases your customer limit. Tier 1 = 500 customers, Tier 2 = 2,000 customers, Tier 3 = unlimited customers. Stack to reach the tier you need.
