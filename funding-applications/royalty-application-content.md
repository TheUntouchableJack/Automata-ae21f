## Business Overview

### Company Name
Royalty (royaltyapp.ai)

### One-Liner
AI-powered loyalty programs for local businesses - describe your business, get a complete loyalty system in 60 seconds.

### Elevator Pitch (100 words)
Royalty is an AI-powered platform that helps local businesses create and run loyalty programs without any technical knowledge. Business owners simply describe their business, and our AI builds a complete loyalty program in under 60 seconds - including rewards, point systems, customer apps, and automated marketing. We're focused on the 33 million small businesses in the US that lose customers to big chains with sophisticated loyalty programs. Royalty levels the playing field by giving every coffee shop, salon, and local restaurant the same powerful tools as Starbucks, but at a price they can afford.

### Problem Statement
Local businesses lose repeat customers to big chains because they can't afford or manage sophisticated loyalty programs. The average small business owner doesn't have time to learn complex software, hire developers, or manage ongoing customer engagement campaigns.

### Solution
Royalty uses AI to:
1. **Build instantly** - Describe your business in plain English, AI creates your entire loyalty program
2. **Run automatically** - AI handles customer engagement, birthday campaigns, win-back messaging
3. **Grow intelligently** - AI analyzes customer behavior and recommends actions to increase retention

### Business Model
- **SaaS subscription** targeting SMBs
- **AppSumo Lifetime Deal launch** February 28, 2026 ($500K target)
- **Visits-based model** - customers earn points through check-ins, not purchases (simpler for SMBs)

### Technology Stack
- Vanilla HTML/CSS/JavaScript (lightweight, fast)
- Supabase (database, auth, real-time)
- Claude API (AI intelligence layer)
